About Taurus Carpentry Solutions Ltd.

We are a local established company based in Hertfordshire specialising in all aspects of carpentry and building work. We cover the Hertfordshire & Bedfordshire area as well as North London.

We are a newly established company, although have years of experience working in the carpentry and building business. Customer satisfaction is the core of our business and we will ensure that your work is completed with maximum satisfaction and minimum fuss and disruption to your daily life.

We have worked on residential and commercial property and no job is too large or small. Our operatives are City & Guilds qualified and hold the relevant CSCS cards.

All our work is of the highest quality and we have the touch and finesse for detailed projects as well as the more rugged skills required to add a dorma to a loft conversion or knock through a kitchen to a dining room.

We are a trustworthy company aiming for total customer satisfaction and want to continue building our reputation by providing a high standard of workmanship, customer service and competitive pricing.

Please take a minute to look through our website and portfolio of work. If you would like more information on our services or would like to have a free no obligation quote then do not hesitate to contact us.
